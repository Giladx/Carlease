Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ending your lease

Can I end my lease early?

Yes! We can help you end your lease early without traditional early termination fees. We work directly with leasing companies to negotiate the best terms for you.

What if I'm over my mileage limit?

Don't worry! Excess mileage fees can be factored into our offer. In many cases, we can help you avoid paying those fees directly to the leasing company.

Do you handle all the paperwork?

Absolutely! We communicate directly with your leasing company and handle all transfer paperwork, title work, and payoff documentation. You just provide us with the details.

How long does the process take?

Most lease-end transitions are completed within 48-72 hours after inspection. We work quickly to ensure a smooth and efficient process.

What if there's damage to the vehicle?

We assess all damage during our inspection. Minor wear and tear is normal and expected. We'll give you a fair valuation based on the actual condition.

Can I lease a new vehicle from you?

Yes! We can help you transition from your current lease into a brand new vehicle. Browse our inventory of 202+ vehicles and get started today.
